How To Reach Nanded?

Transportation by Road: Nanded is connected to all major cities by means of rail,road and air. Nanded is 650 KM east of Mumbai by road and about six-hour drive from Aurangabad and Hyderabad and 11-hour drive from Pune. Several passenger bus services operate from Nanded providing easy overnight connectivity with almost every major city in Maharashtra. These buses are reasonably priced and fairly punctual. The comfort level differs greatly from operator to operator.
Transportation by Railways : Direct passenger trains are available to Nanded from Mumbai, Aurangabad , Hyderabad , Bangalore, Nagpur , Patna , Delhi , Pune and Amritsar . Nanded is one of the Divisional Headquarters of South Central Railway.
